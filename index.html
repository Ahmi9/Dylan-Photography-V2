About Pratheep

The Heart Behind the Lens

I'm Pratheep — the photographer and visual storyteller behind Dylan Photography. Born into a culture that celebrates love with unmatched colour, music, and tradition, I bring a deep personal understanding to every multicultural wedding I photograph.

After 300+ weddings across Toronto, South Asia, and around the world, I've built my craft around one truth: your wedding day is the most important story you'll ever live — and it deserves to be told cinematically, honestly, and timelessly.
